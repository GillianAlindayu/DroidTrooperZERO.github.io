About the Creator

George Lucas

George Lucas is an iconic American director, screenwriter, and producer, renowned for creating the legendary Star Wars and Indiana Jones film series.

Lucas also founded Lucasfilm, THX, and Industrial Light & Magic, pushing the boundaries of film production technology. His work has profoundly influenced the cinema industry.
