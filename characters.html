Star Wars Characters Album

Discover the most popular characters from Star Wars, presented in an interactive album format.

Anakin Skywalker

Anakin Skywalker had the potential to become one of the most powerful Jedi ever.

Obi-Wan Kenobi

A legendary Jedi Master, Obi-Wan was noble and skilled in the ways of the Force.

Luke Skywalker

Luke rose from humble beginnings to become one of the greatest Jedi.

Master Yoda

Yoda was a wise and powerful Jedi Master who trained Jedi for over 800 years.

Han Solo

Han was a smuggler-turned-hero who fought for the Rebel Alliance.

Leia Organa

Leia was a leader of the Rebel Alliance and a symbol of hope for the galaxy.

Kylo Ren

The son of Han Solo and Leia Organa, Ben Solo was seduced by the dark side of the Force.

Darth Vader

Once a Jedi, Darth Vader became one of the most feared Sith Lords in history.

Darth Maul

Maul is a powerful Sith Lord, known for his fearsome appearance and skills in battle.
