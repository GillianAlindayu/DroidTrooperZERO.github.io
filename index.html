About

The Star Wars saga is a cultural phenomenon that has captivated audiences since the release of the first film, "Star Wars: Episode IV - A New Hope," in 1977. Created by George Lucas, the franchise spans multiple movies, TV shows, books, and video games, all set in a galaxy far, far away.

The original trilogy, consisting of "A New Hope," "The Empire Strikes Back," and "Return of the Jedi," follows the journey of Luke Skywalker, Princess Leia, and Han Solo as they battle the evil Galactic Empire led by Darth Vader. The prequel trilogy, released between 1999 and 2005, explores the rise of Darth Vader and the fall of the Galactic Republic. The sequel trilogy, which began with "The Force Awakens" in 2015, continues the story with new characters like Rey, Finn, and Poe Dameron, while also bringing back beloved characters from the original trilogy.

In addition to the main saga, standalone films like "Rogue One" and "Solo" provide deeper insights into the Star Wars universe. The franchise also includes animated series such as "The Clone Wars" and "Rebels," which expand on the lore and introduce new characters and storylines.

Star Wars is known for its iconic characters, epic space battles, and the timeless struggle between the light and dark sides of the Force. It has inspired countless fans and has become a cornerstone of modern pop culture.

Our Lore

Star Wars lore is a complex and diverse tapestry of stories, each telling a different story about the Star Wars universe. The lore has been told through various media, including books, films, comic books, and video games. Below are some key points about the Star Wars lore:

Jedi

The Jedi Order

A noble order of protectors unified by their ability to tap into the power of the force. The Jedi Order were the guardians of peace and justice in the Galactic Republic. The age of the Jedi would end, however, due to a nefarious plot by the Sith Lord Darth Sidious.

  • The Jedi Order was established by the Galactic Empire, led by Darth Vader, in 1977.
  • The Jedi Master was the highest rank within the Jedi Order, and they were responsible for guiding the galaxy.
  • The Jedi Order was divided into three branches: the Sith, the Emperor's Order, and the Dark Side.
  • The Dark Side was a force that sought to control the Force and maintain balance within the galaxy.

Dark Side

The Sith Empire

The dark side of the Force was defined as anger, fear, aggression and a lust for power. It was stated that the Sith referred to the dark side as "dark" because it was secretive. The Sith kept their knowledge and identities secret because they believed it would make it easier for them to impose order.

  • The Dark Side was a force that sought to control the Force and maintain balance within the galaxy.
  • The Dark Side was created by the Sith, led by Darth Sidious, in 1977.
  • The Dark Side was a powerful force that was used to manipulate and control the Force, but it also brought about chaos and destruction.
  • The Dark Side was divided into two main branches: the Force Awakens and the Rebellion.
